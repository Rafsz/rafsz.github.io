In the past 12months RAPT4 dropped from R$14 to R$11, that is: -21.5% .¶

While RAPT3 went from R$10.50 to 13, that is: +24%.¶

Maybe the reason is the Dividends Paid for each stock?¶

4. Checking Dividends paid in the past 12 months:¶

However, both stocks paid R$0.6491 in the past 12 months...¶

5. Conclusion:¶

Considering both stocks paid the same amount of dividends in the past 12 months, RAPT3 was by far a better investment than RAPT4!¶

Why?...¶

Supposing the company anounced that it would start paying the same amount of dividends for both stock, it would make sense for their prices to converge; so both stocks would have the same Dividend Yield.¶

However, this is not what happened, RAPT3 is clearly over-valuated when compared to RAPT4. The final 3 on the ticker means this stock gives voting rights, but it does not justify such a valuation difference.¶

Unless there are some investor with inside information about a possible future offer to buy this company's control...¶

--> My conclusion is that there is a lack of transparency here...¶
